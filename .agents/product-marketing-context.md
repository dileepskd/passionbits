# Passionbits — GTM & Outbound Intelligence Context

## 1. Product Summary

Passionbits is a creative intelligence + video production platform that tells you **what to make, why to make it, and then makes it** — using competitor data, your own ad experiment history, and a global creator network of 6,000+.

### Three Layers

1. **See** — Competitor ad intelligence (what formats, hooks, creators competitors are using)
2. **Think** — Experiment analysis (what's working in your own ad history, what to kill, what to double down on)
3. **Make** — Video production at scale via global creator network

**The core unlock:** Most brands are either flying blind (no data) or data-rich but production-poor. Passionbits closes both gaps in one platform.

- Lives in dashboards — ROAS, CTR, CPM, frequency
- Thinks in experiments — always testing, always iterating
- **Biggest fear:** Budget wasted on creatives that don't convert

- **Biggest fear:** Losing a client because results plateaued
- **Biggest frustration:** Producing creatives at scale without burning out the team
- **What they want to hear:** "This makes your team 3x more productive per client"
- **Decision trigger:** A tool that makes them look smarter to their clients

## 5. Common Objections & Responses

**"We already use Meta Ads Manager for insights"**
Meta shows you your own data. It doesn't show you what your competitors are running, what hooks they're testing, or what formats are working in your category. That's the gap we fill.

**"We have an agency handling our creatives"**
Agencies are great at execution but they're often guessing on strategy. We give you the intelligence layer so your agency always knows what to produce next — and you stop paying for creative experiments that were never going to work.

**"We don't have budget right now"**
The brands spending the most on ads are the ones who can least afford to produce the wrong creatives. One insight that redirects your creative strategy pays for the platform many times over.

**"Can't Claude or AI tools do this?"**
Claude can connect to your Meta account and analyse your own ads. But it has zero competitor data. It doesn't know what Mamaearth or Sugar are running this week. That's our data layer — and that's what makes the difference.

**"We produce enough content already"**
Volume isn't the problem — direction is. If you're producing 20 videos a month without knowing which formats your competitors are doubling down on, you're leaving a lot of performance on the table.

**"We're not sure about the quality of UGC creators"**
Our network of 6,000+ creators is vetted and categorised by niche, format, and geography. You get creator recommendations based on what's actually performing in your category right now — not a random marketplace browse.

## 9. Brand Voice

**Tone:** Confident, founder-to-founder, data-backed but human
**Not:** Corporate, salesy, feature-dumping, jargon-heavy

### Voice Principles

- Lead with insight, not product
- Speak like a smart friend who happens to have data your prospect doesn't
- Make the prospect feel like they're missing something specific, not generic
- Use their category and their competitors' names where possible — make it personal
- Short sentences. Direct. No fluff.

**On-brand example:**
> "Your competitors ran 47 video ads last month. You ran 12. Here's what they tested that you haven't."

**Off-brand example:**
> "Our AI-powered creative intelligence platform leverages proprietary data to optimise your video ad performance at scale."
