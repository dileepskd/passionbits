## Source Notes

1. **Inc42 D2C coverage** (inc42.com/tag/d2c) — primary source for Indian D2C funding + revenue.
2. **Entrackr funding tracker** (entrackr.com) — fastest ROC-filing-based revenue numbers. Search `[brand] FY24 revenue`.
3. **Tracxn India D2C dashboard** — most complete D2C list; filter by stage/revenue/category.
4. **Honasa DRHP + annual report** — real P&Ls for Mamaearth, Derma Co, Aqualogica, BBlunt, Ayuga.
5. **Meta Ad Library India** (facebook.com/ads/library) — hard signal. Filter country=India, active ads, video. The top 200 video advertisers here IS the prospect list.
6. **Fireside Ventures portfolio** — India's leading consumer VC; their portfolio is essentially a curated Passionbits ICP list.
7. **Shark Tank India per-season pages** (SonyLIV + Wikipedia) — S3 and S4 breakouts.
8. **Redseer D2C reports** + **RedBrick Labs D2C Insider** — category growth data.
9. **The Ken / The Morning Context** — deep dives on Good Glamm, Honasa, Licious, Mamaearth, Sugar; qualifies which "growing" brands are actually healthy.
10. **CapTable** — post-Mamaearth IPO landscape coverage.
