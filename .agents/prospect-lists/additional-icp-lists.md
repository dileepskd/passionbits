## How to use these lists

**Immediate action path:**
1. Open **Meta Ad Library** in Chrome
2. For each brand, search the brand name → filter "All active ads" → count video ads
3. 50+ active video ads = active outreach target
4. <50 = deprioritize or re-check in 30 days

**Outreach prioritization matrix (suggested):**

| Segment | Volume | Deal size | Close speed | Prio |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Indian D2C ₹10Cr+ | High | Medium | Fast | 🔥 1 |
| Shark Tank India brands | Medium | Medium | Fast (warm) | 🔥 2 |
| Perf agencies (India) | Low | High (multi-brand) | Medium | 🔥 3 |
| Global D2C Meta spenders | High | High | Slower | 4 |
| Indians going global | Medium | High | Medium | 5 |
| Organic content scalers | Medium | Medium | Fast | 6 |
| B2B event exhibitors | Low | Low (event-bound) | Fast | 7 |
| Global perf agencies | Low | Very high | Slow | 8 |

**Skills to run next (from the installed skill library):**
- `competitor-intel` on the top 10 brands in each list → get leverage strategies
- `outreach-specialist` → craft cold emails/LinkedIn for each segment
- `strategic-planning` → prioritize which segment to attack first based on current ICP fit
- `marketing-ideas` → generate campaign angles per segment
